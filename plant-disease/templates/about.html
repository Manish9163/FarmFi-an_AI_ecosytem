About PlantGuard

Empowering Farmers and Gardeners with AI Technology

🎯 Our Mission

PlantGuard is an AI-powered plant disease detection system designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals quickly identify plant diseases and receive actionable treatment recommendations. We leverage cutting-edge deep learning technology to provide accurate disease diagnosis in seconds.

Our goal is to make plant disease management accessible to everyone, from large-scale farmers to home gardeners, helping reduce crop losses and promote sustainable agriculture.

🔧 Technology Stack

PlantGuard is built using state-of-the-art technologies:

  • 📚 Framework: TensorFlow & Keras
  • 🧠 Model: MobileNetV3 Architecture
  • 🌐 Backend: Flask (Python)
  • 💻 Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • 📦 Dataset: PlantVillage Dataset
  • 🎨 UI/UX: Responsive Design

🔍 How It Works

  1. Upload Image: Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf
  2. AI Analysis: Our model analyzes the image using deep learning
  3. Disease Identification: System identifies the disease with confidence score
  4. Recommendations: Get symptoms, causes, prevention, and treatment solutions
  5. Take Action: Implement the recommended treatments to save your plants

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Good Lighting: Use natural daylight for clear image capture
  • Focus on Affected Areas: Zoom in on diseased parts of the leaf
  • Clear Background: Remove clutter and use a neutral background
  • Fill the Frame: Ensure the leaf occupies most of the image
  • Multiple Angles: If unsure, try uploading images from different angles
  • Clean Lens: Ensure your camera lens is clean for sharp images

⚠️ Disclaimer

PlantGuard is an AI-based diagnostic tool and should not be used as a substitute for professional agricultural advice. While our model is trained on thousands of images, we recommend consulting with a professional agronomist or plant pathologist for critical decisions affecting your crops. Always verify results and consider multiple sources before implementing treatment plans.
